# 🌌 Dream Capsule

### Memories Sealed. Legacy Revealed.

Dream Capsule is an immersive digital experience that transforms campus life into a mystical journey through memories, friendships, achievements, culture, and dreams.

Inspired by the concept of a Shadow Realm, the website presents student experiences as timeless echoes preserved within a magical Dream Capsule, where every memory contributes to a lasting legacy.

---

## ✨ Project Overview

Campus life is more than classrooms and examinations. It is a collection of moments, emotions, challenges, achievements, friendships, celebrations, and dreams.

Dream Capsule visualizes these experiences through:

* AI-generated artwork
* AI-generated music
* Interactive storytelling
* Cinematic animations
* Immersive visual effects

The project invites visitors to relive the journey of student life and reflect on the memories that shape their future.

---

## 🌌 About Dream Capsule

Dream Capsule is a cinematic digital experience that transforms campus life into a realm of memories, dreams, and legacy. Every friendship, achievement, challenge, celebration, and milestone is preserved as an everlasting echo within the capsule.

Built with a Shadow Realm-inspired aesthetic, the project combines AI-generated visuals, immersive music, interactive storytelling, and modern web animations to create an emotional journey through student life.

Rather than simply showcasing events, Dream Capsule captures what remains after the journey ends—the memories that shape us and the legacy we leave behind.
